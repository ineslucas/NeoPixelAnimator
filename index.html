Part 1 · Place

Tap to place your lights.

Each tap drops one NeoPixel onto the canvas. Drag a placed light to reposition. The order you place them becomes the strip's wiring index. 0, 1, 2...

0 lights placed

Part 2 · Sequence

Tap them in order.

The click order is how the animation walks through the lights. Tap an LED to add it to the sequence. Tap again to remove. Or hit "Use placement order" to fill it in 0, 1, 2...

0 in sequence

Part 3 · Style

Make them flow.

Tune speed, color, and trail length. The preview runs the same algorithm the Arduino code will.

Or pick a preset

Part 4 · Export

Get your code.

Paste this into the Arduino IDE. Make sure the Adafruit_NeoPixel library is installed. Adjust LED_PIN to match your wiring.
